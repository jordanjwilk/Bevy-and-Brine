JONATHAN NIMERFROH X BEVY AND BRINE

Jonathan Nimerfroh and Brooke Kelley met in 2018 while surfing at San Onofre in Southern California. They share a love for surf culture and a laid back lifestyle. This curated collection of photography was created in Mexico, Nantucket, the Caribbean, Maine and California. Hand-painted by Brooke on the island of Kauai, Jonathan’s photography is well-complimented by Brooke’s immense talents to make for a truly unique and beautiful collection of art. This collaboration is exclusively available only at the Samual Owen Gallery here. Email Bevyandbrine@gmail.com for inquiries
